About EduQuest

EduQuest creates playful, effective learning experiences for Norwegian students through browser-based educational games and tools.

Our mission is to make studying more engaging by combining proven educational methods with modern gamification techniques.

All tools are built with accessibility and the Norwegian curriculum in mind.

Norwegian Curriculum
Aligned with NTNU and Norwegian school standards
Gamification
Streaks, XP, levels and rewards that keep students motivated
Browser-Based
No downloads needed. Works on any device.
